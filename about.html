Our Craftsmanship Process

Sourcing

We carefully source premium natural ingredients, essential oils, and sustainable materials from trusted suppliers.

Blending

Expert aromatherapists blend custom fragrance combinations that evoke Indian traditions and modern sensibilities.

Hand-Pouring

Each candle is hand-poured at optimal temperatures to ensure even burn, beautiful colors, and perfect texture.

Curing

Candles are allowed to cure for 24-48 hours, allowing the fragrance to fully develop for optimal scent throw.

Quality Check

Every candle is inspected for quality, color consistency, and burn performance before packaging.

Packaging

Candles are carefully packaged in eco-friendly materials with detailed care instructions and fragrance information.
