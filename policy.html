Candle Care Instructions

Follow these guidelines to ensure optimal burn quality and maximum fragrance throw from your Samriddhi candles.

Before First Burn

  • Allow the candle to sit for 24 hours after unboxing before lighting.
  • This curing period allows the fragrance to fully develop.

Burning Guidelines

  • First Burn: Light the candle and allow it to burn for 2-3 hours. This creates an even melt pool and prevents tunneling.
  • Burn Time: Never burn a candle for more than 4 hours at a time.
  • Wick Trimming: Trim the wick to ¬º inch (0.6 cm) before each burn to prevent excessive smoking and ensure even burning.
  • Safe Location: Place candles on heat-resistant, level surfaces away from drafts, curtains, and flammable materials.
  • Never Leave Unattended: Always watch burning candles. Never leave a burning candle unattended.
  • Keep Away from Pets & Children: Ensure candles are placed out of reach of children and pets.

Storage

  • Store candles in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Avoid storing in areas with temperature fluctuations.
  • Keep away from heat sources and moisture.
  • Properly stored candles can last for years before use.

Common Issues & Solutions

Tunneling: If the candle melts only in the center, allow it to burn longer during the first burn to create an even melt pool. Trim the wick and try again.

Sooting (Black Residue): Trim the wick to ¬º inch. Ensure the candle is away from drafts. Move it to a draft-free location.

Weak Scent: Ensure the candle has completely cooled before lighting again. Trim the wick and check for drafts.

When to Discontinue Burning

  • Stop burning when ¬Ω inch of wax remains at the bottom.
  • Burning beyond this point can damage the container.
  • If you notice excessive smoking or the flame becomes too high, extinguish and relight.

Reusing Containers

After the candle has burned completely, the glass container can be washed and reused for decoration, storage, or to make your own candles.

Safety Information

Important Safety Warnings

  • Never leave a burning candle unattended.
  • Keep candles away from flammable materials, curtains, and drafts.
  • Extinguish the candle if the flame becomes too high or flickers repeatedly.
  • Do not use candles in bedrooms unless supervised.
  • Keep candles away from children and pets.
  • Never touch the container while lit, as it will be hot.
  • Discontinue use when ¬Ω inch of wax remains.

Allergies & Sensitivities

Our candles are made with natural fragrances and essential oils. If you have fragrance sensitivities or allergies, please perform a patch test or consult the ingredient list before use. If you experience any adverse reactions, discontinue use immediately.

In Case of Accident

  • Burns: If you accidentally get burned, immediately place the affected area under cool running water for 10-15 minutes and seek medical attention if necessary.
  • Fire: If the candle causes a fire, use a fire extinguisher or call emergency services immediately.
  • Ingestion: If someone ingests candle wax, call poison control immediately.

Materials & Ingredients
