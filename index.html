Our Story

SAMRIDDHI AROMA CANDLES was born from a profound belief: that light has the transformative power to elevate spaces, moods, and moments of our lives.

Rooted deeply in ancient Vedic traditions where fire symbolized purity, divinity, and spiritual awakening, we craft each candle as a contemporary homage to timeless wisdom. Every piece is meticulously handmade with conscious intention, blending premium natural waxes, pure therapeutic essential oils, and masterful artistic moulding techniques—skills passed down through generations of authentic Indian craftsmanship.

We are committed to slow living, mindful consumption, and celebrating the authentic beauty of handmade artistry. Our candles transcend mere products; they are rituals of calm, vessels of warmth, and silent companions on your journey toward inner peace and spiritual balance.

Whether you're creating a personal sanctuary at home, honoring a sacred festival, or seeking the perfect meaningful gift, SAMRIDDHI AROMA CANDLES brings the gentle, enchanting glow of tradition into your modern life.

Our Policies

Shipping Policy

We ship across India through trusted courier partners. Most orders are dispatched within 2-3 business days.

  • Free shipping on orders above ₹1,000
  • Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days
  • Express delivery available in select cities (1-3 days)
  • All candles are carefully packaged to prevent damage during transit
  • You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships

Return & Replacement Policy

Your satisfaction matters to us. We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery.

  • Products must be unused and in original packaging
  • Return shipping costs are borne by the customer unless the product is defective
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item
  • Custom orders and gift sets are non-returnable unless damaged
  • Contact us on WhatsApp for return requests
